Funding my training

There are three types of funding available for teacher training. Find out below which you’re eligible for. Register with us for tailored advice on your funding options and support towards applying for teacher training,

1. Bursaries and scholarships

Depending on your degree and subject, you could receive a scholarship of up to £28,000 or a bursary of up to £26,000. Find out more.

2. Tuition Fee and Maintenance Loans

Even if you already have a student loan, you can still apply for tuition fee and maintenance loans to fund your teacher training. Learn more.

3. Extra financial support

There's extra financial support available if you’re a parent, have an adult dependant or a disability.
